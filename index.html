Controls Map:
Play Mode: SPACEBAR, UP ARROW, or Click to Jump. Tap action keys inside Yellow Orbs to launch midair. 'R' to reload.
Editor Mode: Left Click = Draw, Right Click = Erase. Use Left/Right Arrows to scroll map.
New Pan View: Hold down SPACEBAR + Drag Mouse across canvas to rapidly slide camera view left/right.
Object Toggles: Press 'E' on keyboard to rotate the active object brush cleanly by 90° increments before drawing.
